Remember, One's Complement has two representations of 0, +0 and -0. +0 is 00000000, and -0 is 11111111. Two's Complement only has the positive representation (00000000). Both One's Complement and Two's Complement are used to represent negative numbers, so if you input a positive number, its One's Complement and Two's Complement would be the same as its regular binary representation.
